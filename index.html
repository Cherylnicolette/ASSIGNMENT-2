Welcome To Amor!

At Amor, we're more than just a bubble tea company; we're a flavorful journey that combines tradition with innovation. Our passion for crafting the perfect bubble tea experience has led us to become a beloved destination for tea enthusiasts and flavor adventurers alike. Amor is an innovative, artsy, comfortable brand that offers natural, healthy, yet tasty handmade beverages and toppings. Our specialty drinks, unique bottles and modern store delivers a pleasing experience of Asian tea culture.

Premium bubble tea brand with more than 20 locations in Ontario, and 500 stores around the world

Founded in 2017, Amor was born out of a love for authentic, high-quality bubble tea. Our journey began with a simple dream: to share the joy of this beloved beverage with our community. From our very first sip, we were captivated by the artistry of brewing tea, the excitement of experimenting with unique flavors, and the sheer delight that a perfectly crafted bubble tea can bring.

As our passion grew, so did our commitment to excellence. We embarked on a quest to source the finest tea leaves and ingredients from around the world, ensuring that every cup of bubble tea we serve is a masterpiece of flavor and quality.
